About Radio Jingles Ltd

Established in 1996, Radio Jingles Ltd has been producing professional radio commercials, jingles, and station idents for nearly three decades. Based in Torquay, Devon, we serve radio stations and advertisers across the entire United Kingdom.

Our in-house production workflow ensures every piece of audio we deliver is broadcast-ready, polished and produced to the highest standards. Whether you need a single voiced ad or a full advertising campaign, we have the expertise and facilities to deliver.

Behind the Sound of Torbay Radio

Radio Jingles power the on-air sound of Torbay Radio (107.9FM) — the English Riviera's community radio station.

Broadcasting across South Devon on FM and DAB, and worldwide online, Torbay Radio delivers local news, travel, weather and a brilliant music mix from the 60s to today — all driven by passionate local presenters.

It's radio made for the community, by the community.
