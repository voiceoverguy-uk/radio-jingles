About Radio Jingles Ltd

Established in 1996, Radio Jingles Ltd has been producing professional radio commercials, jingles and station idents for nearly three decades.

Based in Torquay, Devon, we work with radio stations, advertising agencies and businesses across the United Kingdom and Gibraltar, creating audio that captures attention and delivers results on-air.

Our experienced scriptwriters craft adverts designed to highlight your brand and message clearly and creatively. Using professional broadcast equipment and industry-standard production tools, our in-house team ensures every commercial we deliver is polished, powerful and ready for airplay.

Whether you need a single voiced commercial, a full radio campaign, or station branding, we have the expertise and facilities to deliver.

Over the years we've produced thousands of commercials and promotions for brands including Coldseal Windows, Wilco Motosave & Fastfit, Mattressman, Trago Mills, Fostering Somerset and many more, along with many independent businesses and radio stations across the UK.

We're incredibly proud of the work we produce and look forward to helping bring your next radio campaign to life.

Behind the Sound of Torbay Radio

Radio Jingles power the on-air sound of Torbay Radio (107.9FM) — the English Riviera's community radio station.

Broadcasting across South Devon on FM and DAB, and worldwide online, Torbay Radio delivers local news, travel, weather and a brilliant music mix from the 60s to today — all driven by passionate local presenters.

It's radio made for the community, by the community.
