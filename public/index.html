Radio Jingles & Commercial Production

Radio Jingles Ltd produce both sung jingles and spoken commercials — everything created from scratch by real writers, real composers and real singers at our fully-equipped Torquay studios. From catchy melodies that define a brand to voiced ads with sound effects, we deliver broadcast-ready audio production for radio stations, agencies and businesses of all sizes.

Sung & Spoken

We produce both fully sung jingles and spoken radio commercials — often combining the two. A partially sung jingle pairs a memorable musical hook with spoken inserts that can be updated throughout the licence period.

Real Singers & Writers

Every production features real professional vocalists — male or female, with harmonies where needed. Our in-house writers handle concept, lyrics and script from brief to approval.

Broadcast-Ready Delivery

All final audio is professionally mixed and mastered to broadcast standards. We deliver WAV and MP3 files, ready for immediate use on any UK radio station or digital platform.

What We Do

Everything you need for professional radio advertising, all under one roof.

Voiced Radio Ads

Professional voiced commercials written, produced and delivered broadcast-ready for any UK radio station. Scripts are checked for Radiocentre Clearance compliance, and revisions are free until you're happy before production begins.

Advertising Jingles

Bespoke sung jingles that make your brand name impossible to forget on air. We write original lyrics, compose original music and produce everything in-house — no templates, no off-the-shelf beds.

Station Idents

Distinctive sung idents, sponsor stings, show openers and promo beds that give your station a polished, professional identity. We can match your existing station sound or create something completely fresh.

Scriptwriting

Original scripts written by our in-house creative team — crafted around your brand, audience and call to action. Revisions are included at no charge until the script is approved by you.

Voice Talent

A wide roster of experienced male and female UK voiceover artists, chosen to match the style, energy and audience of your production. We advise on the best voice for your brief.

Full Campaign Production

End-to-end campaign production from concept through to broadcast delivery — including buying airtime. We work directly with clients as well as through advertising agencies, and can design, produce and manage an entire radio campaign to your budget.

About Radio Jingles Ltd

Established in 1996, Radio Jingles Ltd has been producing professional radio commercials, jingles and station idents for nearly three decades.

Based in Torquay, Devon, we work with radio stations, advertising agencies and businesses across the United Kingdom and Gibraltar, creating audio that captures attention and delivers results on-air.

Our experienced scriptwriters craft adverts designed to highlight your brand and message clearly and creatively. Using professional broadcast equipment and industry-standard production tools, our in-house team ensures every commercial we deliver is polished, powerful and ready for airplay.

Whether you need a single voiced commercial, a full radio campaign, or station branding, we have the expertise and facilities to deliver.

Over the years we've produced thousands of commercials and promotions for brands including Coldseal Windows, Wilco Motosave & Fastfit, Mattressman, Trago Mills, Fostering Somerset and many more, along with many independent businesses and radio stations across the UK.

We're incredibly proud of the work we produce and look forward to helping bring your next radio campaign to life.

Behind the Sound of Torbay Radio

Radio Jingles power the on-air sound of Torbay Radio (107.9FM) — the English Riviera's community radio station.
