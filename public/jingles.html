Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our radio jingle and commercial production services.

What types of radio jingles and commercials do you produce?

We regularly produce both sung jingles and spoken commercials. We also work with many small radio stations across the country to provide sponsorship tags, station idents, show openers and promo beds. Our sung radio jingles range from commercial hooks to full station identity packages, and our spoken commercials are produced to Radiocentre Clearance standard.

Do you create everything from scratch?

Absolutely. Every commercial we produce is created from the ground up — including concept, lyrics, composition and full production. We are proud to have musicians and creative writers as part of our full-time team. Nothing is off-the-shelf or template-based.

How long does production take?

We pride ourselves on our turnaround times. A spoken commercial can go from initial brief to broadcast in as little as two days, if script approval is immediate. Most sung jingle projects are completed within 5–10 working days, depending on complexity and feedback turnaround. Urgent fast-turnaround work can be prioritised. Find out more about our full radio jingle production process.

How does licensing work?

Sung jingles are licensed for two years. A fully sung jingle is used without amendment for that period. A partially sung commercial allows spoken inserts to be updated to suit promotions or events during the licence period — each insert is charged individually. Spoken commercials are licensed for one year. We also offer competitive rates for licensing to other stations and for additional years.

Can I come to you with just a rough idea?

Yes — you can come to us with as little as a rough idea. We'll guide you through the process and help shape it into a strong creative concept. All we need to get started is your brand name, your target audience, what you want listeners to do, and the general feel or style you're after.

Do you work with clients outside Torquay?

Yes. We work with radio stations, advertising agencies and businesses across the whole of the UK and beyond, including Gibraltar. Final broadcast-ready audio is delivered digitally — so geography is no barrier. See also our advertising jingles service for brands and campaign clients.
