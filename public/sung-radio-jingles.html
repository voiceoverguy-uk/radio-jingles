What Are Sung Radio Jingles?

A sung radio jingle is a short, original piece of music — written, composed and performed specifically for your brand or radio station. It combines a memorable melody with a lyric that communicates your key message, delivered by a real professional singer.

Sung jingles are one of the oldest and most effective tools in radio advertising. Before the internet, before streaming, before social media — the jingle was how brands got into people's heads. That hasn't changed. A great melody still cuts through noise in a way that nothing else quite manages.

At Radio Jingles Ltd, we've been producing sung jingles since 1996. Every jingle we create is bespoke — written from scratch for you, performed by real singers, and produced to broadcast quality in our fully-equipped Torquay studios. Sung jingles are one part of our broader radio jingle production service, which also covers station IDs, promo beds, sponsor stings and full advertising campaigns.

Why Melody Matters

The human brain processes music differently from speech. When a brand name or message is delivered with melody, it's encoded in a part of memory that's more durable and emotionally connected. People may forget a voiceover. They'll remember a tune.

That's why the best radio campaigns have always had a musical identity at their core. And that's why — even in the streaming era — a well-crafted sung jingle remains one of the most cost-effective investments in audio branding you can make.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you'd want to know about getting a professional sung jingle made for your brand or radio station.

What are sung radio jingles?

Sung radio jingles are short musical compositions featuring original melodies and lyrics, performed by real singers, used in radio advertising, station branding, sponsor stings and promotional campaigns. A strong melody and memorable lyric can instantly identify a brand and stay with listeners long after the broadcast ends — making them one of the most enduring tools in audio marketing.

Why are sung jingles so effective?

Melody is one of the most powerful memory triggers the human brain processes. When a brand name or slogan is set to a catchy tune and performed by a real singer, it bypasses conscious processing and becomes part of the listener's emotional memory. Audio with melody is recalled far more effectively than spoken words alone — which is why the most memorable brands in radio history have always had a sound as well as a look.

Yes — everything we produce is bespoke and created specifically for you. We don't use off-the-shelf beds or generic templates. Are sung jingles suitable for podcasts and online audio?

Absolutely. Sung jingles are just as effective in digital audio as they are on traditional radio.
