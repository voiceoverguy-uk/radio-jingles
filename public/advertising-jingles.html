What Are Advertising Jingles?

An advertising jingle is a short, original piece of music — usually sung — created specifically to promote a brand, product or campaign. It pairs a simple message with a melody that lodges in the listener's mind and keeps returning long after the advert has finished playing.

The format isn't new, but it is still one of the most effective tools in audio advertising. When a melody carries a brand name or a campaign message, the brain stores it in a different way than it does spoken words. That's not nostalgia talking — it's how memory works. And in a crowded media landscape where attention is short, an advertising jingle that connects emotionally with its audience has a real commercial advantage.

At Radio Jingles Ltd, we've been writing and producing advertising jingles since 1996. Every project is handled in-house, from the first conversation about your brief to the final broadcast-ready audio. Our radio jingle production service covers the full creative and technical process, and our team includes experienced writers, composers, singers and audio engineers.

Made for Real Campaigns

Advertising jingles are not old-fashioned filler. Produced well, they are a form of strategic audio branding — a melody that becomes inseparable from the brand it represents. Think of the most famous examples and you'll notice they don't just sell a product; they become part of the cultural landscape around it.

We don't produce generic or template-based audio. Every advertising jingle we create is written and composed specifically for the client, their audience and their campaign goals. If the brief calls for a warm, melodic hook, that's what gets made. If it needs energy and attitude, we go there instead. The brief drives everything.

Why Advertising Jingles Work

A well-crafted advertising jingle does something no voiceover alone can do — it makes the message musical, and musical messages are the ones that endure.

Stronger Brand Recall

Research consistently shows that melody-based advertising improves brand recall significantly compared with spoken messaging alone. A good jingle makes your brand name impossible to forget.

More Memorable Campaigns

Listeners may hear dozens of ads in a day and remember almost none of them. An advertising jingle that catches is the exception — it creates a moment that registers and returns.

Emotional Connection

Music creates emotional responses that plain speech cannot. A jingle that feels right for a brand builds warmth and familiarity with the audience over time, not just recognition.

Campaign Consistency

A strong jingle can run across multiple ad formats, cuts and durations without going stale. It holds a campaign together sonically and gives every execution an immediate identity.

Standing Out on Air

Most radio advertising is voiceover over a generic music bed. A genuine advertising jingle — with a real hook and a distinctive melody — naturally commands more attention in that context.

Messages That Stick

When the key message of a campaign is set to music, listeners carry it with them. That's the ultimate goal of advertising — and a well-made jingle delivers it in under ten seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Useful answers to common questions about our advertising jingle production service.

What is an advertising jingle?

An advertising jingle is a short, original piece of music — usually sung — created to promote a brand, product or campaign. It combines a catchy melody with a simple message designed to stay in the listener's mind. Advertising jingles are used in radio commercials, TV ads, online audio, sponsor tags and promotional content of all kinds.

Are jingles still effective in advertising?

Yes — arguably more so in a crowded audio landscape. Research consistently shows that melody-based messaging improves brand recall significantly compared with spoken ads alone. A well-crafted advertising jingle cuts through, stays in the memory long after the broadcast ends, and creates an emotional connection with the brand that a voiceover simply cannot replicate.

Can you create a custom jingle for my brand?

Yes — every jingle we produce is original and built around your brief from scratch. We write the lyrics, compose the melody and produce the audio ourselves. There are no templates, no shared music libraries and no off-the-shelf beds. Your jingle is unique to you.

Do you use real singers?

Always. We work with professional UK singers with wide-ranging styles and real performance experience. We do not use AI-generated vocals or synthesised voices. Every sung performance is delivered by a real person, and the difference is clearly audible in the finished audio.

Can you produce jingles for radio ads and promotions?

Yes. Radio advertising jingles are at the heart of what we do. We produce everything from a single 10-second sung sting to a full jingle campaign with multiple variations, different cuts and sponsor tags. All audio is delivered broadcast-ready to the format you need.

Can you make short sung hooks or sponsor tags?

Yes. Short sponsor tags and sung brand hooks are one of our most popular requests. A well-produced three or four-second musical sting with a brand name can be remarkably powerful when it runs consistently across a campaign — and we can produce them as part of a wider package or as standalone audio.

How long does the process take?

Turnaround depends on the type and complexity of the project. A spoken commercial can go from brief to broadcast in as little as two days if script approval is immediate. Most sung advertising jingle projects are completed within 5–10 working days. Larger campaign packages take longer — but timelines are always agreed upfront. If you have a hard deadline, let us know and we'll work to it.

How is an advertising jingle licensed?

Sung advertising jingles are licensed for two years. A fully sung jingle is used as-produced for the duration of the licence. A partially sung jingle allows spoken inserts — such as specific offers, dates or calls to action — to be updated throughout the licence period, making the jingle commercially flexible. Spoken-only commercials are licensed for one year. Licensing to additional radio stations and extended licence terms are also available.

Can the spoken part of a jingle be updated without re-recording everything?

Yes — if your jingle is produced as a partially sung commercial, the spoken insert can be updated separately throughout the licence period. This means you can update prices, offers or calls to action without re-recording the sung part. Each insert update is a separate production and is charged individually. It's one of the most cost-effective ways to get the best of both formats. See our sung radio jingles page for more detail on this format.

You can come to us with as little as a rough idea — we'll help shape it from there. At a minimum, we need your brand name, what you want the jingle to communicate, your target audience and any preferences on tone or style.
