More Than a Catchy Tune

Radio jingle production is the full creative and technical process of turning a brief into broadcast-ready audio. It covers strategy and creative direction, lyric writing, melody composition, vocal performance, music production and final mastering.

A professionally produced jingle becomes part of your brand identity. Listeners hear it repeatedly, associate it with your company or station, and — when it's done right — find it impossible to get out of their heads. That's the commercial value of investing in quality jingle production rather than settling for something generic or off-the-shelf.

At Radio Jingles Ltd, we've been managing the whole process in-house since 1996. Our writers, composers, singers and engineers work together to deliver something that sounds polished, sits well on air, and does its job from the very first broadcast.

Built to Last

The best jingles outlast individual campaigns. They become associated with a business or station the way a logo does — except audio has the advantage of being impossible to ignore when it plays. You don't have to be looking at a screen for a jingle to work on you.

We work with radio stations, advertisers, brands, agencies, podcast producers and event promoters across the UK. Whatever the brief, we approach every project with the same combination of creative discipline and technical rigour that has made our productions a fixture on UK radio for three decades.

Why Professional Radio Jingle Production Matters

There is a meaningful difference between audio that fills airtime and audio that builds brand equity. Professional jingle production is what makes the difference.

Brand Recognition

A professionally produced jingle creates an immediate audio identifier — something listeners associate with your brand or station the moment it plays, before they even hear the words.

Listener Recall

Melody is one of the brain's strongest memory anchors. A well-crafted jingle gets remembered long after the broadcast ends — far longer than a straight voiceover ever would.

Polished, Broadcast-Ready Sound

Quality production means your audio sits correctly in the broadcast environment — the right levels, the right sonic character, the right energy for the format and audience.

Campaign Consistency

A strong jingle can run across commercials, sponsor stings and promotions without feeling tired, building a coherent audio identity that compounds over time rather than fading.

Credibility on Air

Professional audio signals a professional business. Poorly produced audio does exactly the opposite — regardless of how good the script is or how compelling the offer.

Real Creative Impact

Generic template jingles don't create the same response as something made specifically for you. Bespoke production — with original writing and a real performance — always outperforms off-the-shelf.

How the Production Process Works

Every project is different, but the process follows a clear path — from your first conversation to final broadcast-ready audio.

Step 01

Brief

We start by understanding your brand, station, audience, tone and objectives. The clearer the brief, the better the creative output — and we'll help you shape it if needed.

Step 02

Concept & Writing

Our writers develop the creative direction — the hook, the angle, the lyric approach — before a note is composed. Getting the words right first makes everything else easier.

Step 03

Melody & Composition

Our composers build an original melody around the concept. Genre, tempo and tone are chosen to suit your target audience and the context in which the jingle will be heard.

Step 04

Vocals & Performance

A professional singer or voiceover artist is selected and briefed. The session is produced in-house at our Torquay studios, using real performers — not synthesised or AI-generated voices.

Step 05

Production & Mix

Everything comes together — vocals, music, effects and timing are all balanced, mixed and mastered for broadcast. The result is clean, loud-ready and technically correct.

Step 06

Final Delivery

You receive broadcast-ready audio in the format you need. Revisions are handled quickly and without fuss. We don't consider the job done until you're happy with what you have.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our radio jingle production service — answered clearly.

What is radio jingle production?

Radio jingle production is the full creative and technical process of turning a brief into a broadcast-ready piece of audio. It covers strategy, lyric writing, melody composition, vocal performance, music production and final mastering. The result is a short, memorable audio piece — a jingle, ident, sting or show opener — that can be used in radio broadcasts, advertising campaigns, station imaging and digital audio.

Can you create a jingle completely from scratch?

Yes — everything we produce is original. We write the lyrics, compose the melody, arrange the music and produce the final audio from the ground up. We don't use template tracks or off-the-shelf beds. Your jingle is unique to you, built around your brief from day one.

Do you use real singers?

Always. We work with a trusted roster of professional UK singers across a wide range of styles. We don't use AI-generated vocals or synthesised voices. Every vocal performance on every jingle we produce is delivered by a real person with real skill — and it shows in the final result.

Can you produce jingles for advertising campaigns?

Yes. Commercial advertising jingles are a core part of what we do. Whether you need a single sung jingle for a radio commercial, a package of related jingles for a longer campaign, or sponsor stings and promo beds, we can produce the full range to broadcast specification. For more detail, see our page on sung radio jingles.

Do you make jingles for radio stations and podcasts?

Yes to both. Radio station jingle production — including station IDs, show openers, sponsor stings and promo beds — has been at the heart of our work since 1996. We also produce jingles for podcasts, streaming services and branded digital audio content. The production quality is the same regardless of the platform.

How long does radio jingle production take?

It depends on the complexity of the project, but we work quickly. A straightforward brief can typically be turned around within a few days of receiving your brief and approval. Larger packages or more complex productions take longer, but we always discuss timelines upfront and stick to them. If you have a hard deadline, tell us and we'll work to it.

What do you need from me to get started?

A brief is all you need to begin — the name of your business or station, what you want the jingle to communicate, the style or feel you're aiming for, and any specific requirements around length or format.
