## Real Team Information to Integrate Into the Site
Use the following real service details throughout the site where appropriate, naturally and without stuffing:

### Core service truths
- Radio commercial production includes both spoken commercials and sung jingles
- Sung jingles often combine music, vocals and voiceover
- Spoken commercials may include sound effects
- Both are designed to be memorable and emotionally engaging
- Everything is created from scratch: concept, lyrics, composition and production
- The team includes full-time musicians and creative writers
- They use real singers, vocalists, composers and producers
- They tailor each jingle to the client’s brand, audience and goals
- They produce sponsor tags, idents, intros, promos and other audio assets
- They create work for radio stations, brands, agencies, podcasts, streaming and digital campaigns
- They can match an existing station sound or create something completely fresh
- They offer both male and female vocals
- They can create harmonies and layered vocals
- They can combine sung and spoken sections

### Process / delivery truths
- Clients can come with just a rough idea
- The team helps shape that into a creative concept
- Typical process: brief -> concept -> scripting -> composition -> recording -> production -> mixing -> delivery
- For spoken commercials, script revisions are free until approval before production
- Work is checked for Radiocentre Clearance compliance where appropriate
- Final files are delivered as WAV and/or MP3
- Final audio is broadcast-ready and professionally mixed/mastered
- Spoken commercials can sometimes go from brief to broadcast in 2 days if script approval is immediate
- Most sung jingle projects take around 5–10 working days depending on complexity and feedback
- Urgent fast-turnaround work can be prioritised

### Licensing / commercial truths
- Sung jingles are licensed for 2 years
- Partially sung commercials allow spoken inserts to be updated during the licence period
- Spoken commercials are licensed for 1 year
- Licensing can extend to other stations and additional years
- Usage affects pricing
- They can also support direct clients with airtime buying and full campaign production

### Positioning / trust truths
- Established in 1996
- Bespoke production
- Real singers, real writers, real producers
- Premium, radio-ready sound
- Work with radio stations, agencies, brands and content creators
- Work with local, national and international clients
- Strong on originality, musicality, brand alignment and memorability

IMPORTANT:
Use these truths where they fit naturally.
Do NOT dump them all into one paragraph like a panicked salesman.
